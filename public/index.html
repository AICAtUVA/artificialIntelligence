About Us

The Artificial Intelligence Club’s mission is to teach UVA students about AI and its real-world applications. Members participate in hackathons together and explore the ethics of new technology. Members plan the AI Summit, which is in April, which encourages students to learn more about Artificial Intelligence. The club will also start working on a collective team project using Artificial Intelligence soon.

Keynote Speakers

May Casterline - Senior Architect at Nvidia

May Casterline is an image scientist and software developer at Nvidia and has specific interests and skills in deep learning, novel modeling and visualization techniques for data exploitation, and innovative machine learning applications.

Chris Eichelberger – Senior Software Engineer at CCRI

Chris Eichelberger is a software engineer at CCRI who specializes in machine learning, data mining, parallel computing, and predictive modeling.

Michael Freenor – Senior Data Scientist at WillowTree Apps

Michael Freenor is a data scientist who specializes in big data analysis, algorithms, machine learning, causal inference, and artificial intelligence. He has experienced doing research at the University of Virginia and is also a part time lecturer there.

Samuel Lester – Data Scientist at Microsoft

Samuel Lester has worked at Microsoft for 17 years in a variety of technical roles. Currently, he works as a delivery data scientist specializing in Machine Learning and data science for Microsoft’s largest customers.
